What can I help you with?

By Neil Greaney

As an expert in the field with accreditation from ISESA, I bring a unique blend of experience in both individual and team sports, along with a background in high-level athletics and football. This diverse skill set allows me to offer specialized services to help athletes unlock their full potential and achieve peak performance.

Through my years of experience working with athletes from various sports backgrounds, I have developed a deep understanding of the psychological factors that can impact performance. Whether it's overcoming mental blocks, managing performance anxiety, or improving focus and motivation, I have the tools and expertise to help athletes navigate the mental aspect of their game. My approach is tailored to each individual client, taking into account their unique strengths, challenges, and goals.

By incorporating proven techniques from psychology, such as visualization, goal setting, and mindfulness, I work with athletes to enhance their mental skills and optimize their performance on the field or court.

Team Sports

In addition to working with individual athletes, I also have experience in team sports, where dynamics and communication play a crucial role in success. By addressing team cohesion, leadership, and communication strategies, I help teams perform at their best collectively.

Who I Work With

Whether you are a professional athlete looking to take your game to the next level or a young athlete seeking to build a strong mental foundation for success, my services are designed to help you reach your full potential.

If you are ready to unlock your peak performance and take your game to the next level, I invite you to book a session with me. Together, we can work towards achieving your goals and maximizing your potential in sports.
