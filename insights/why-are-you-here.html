Why are you here?

By Neil Greaney

As individuals and teams strive to excel in their athletic endeavours, many factors come into play beyond physical training, technical development, tactical learning and natural talent. Psychology has gained recognition as a vital component in maximizing one's performance and reaching their full potential in both sports and other performance. Understanding the mental aspect of sports & performance can lead to improved focus, confidence, and overall well-being. That's where psychologists come in to help athletes navigate the mental challenges that often accompany competitive sports & performance.

Are you:

  • An athlete who struggles to cope with setbacks?
  • A coach looking to develop effective communication skills with your team?
  • A parent concerned about the wellbeing and future participation of your child in sport?
  • A performer looking to understand and enhance your confidence?

To only mention a few, if any of the above resonate with you, you have come to the right place. I have experience working with athletes, coaches, parents and performers in developing self-awareness and psychological skills to maximise enjoyment and performance in both sport and life.

Every person presents a different challenge, and it is my mission to create, and guide you through, a personalised programme that works for you. One that helps you to achieve the process and performance that gives you fulfilment.
